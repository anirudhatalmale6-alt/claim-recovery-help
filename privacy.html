Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Information We Collect

When you submit our case review form, we collect your name, phone number, email address, zip code, and information related to your property damage claim including the type of damage and the status of your insurance claim.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to evaluate your potential claim and to connect you with a qualified claims recovery specialist. We may contact you by phone, email, or text message regarding your case submission.

Information Sharing

We may share your information with affiliated claims recovery professionals, public adjusters, and attorneys for the purpose of evaluating and potentially representing your claim. We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies and Tracking

We use Google Analytics and Google Ads conversion tracking to understand how visitors interact with our website and to measure the effectiveness of our advertising. These services may use cookies to collect anonymous usage data. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information, including SSL encryption for all data transmitted through our website. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your data rights, please contact us through the information provided on our website or call us at (866) 378-1405.
